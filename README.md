# Title of research
The title of the research was training needs for registered midwives in two Comprehensive emergency obstetrics and neonatal care facilities
# Purpose
The purpose was to determine trainings that would inform a training programme for registered midwives
## Data collection tool
A Hennessy-Hicks Questionnaire was used to collect data from N=202 registered midwives. The tool had 37 items measured on a seven point Likert Scale of task importance and task performance. The items are grouped into five categories also called superordinate categories. Part of Section C of the tool required open-ended responses. 
## Data analysis
The tool has four sections apart from the demographic characteristics. Sections A,B,C and D. If indicator B (perceived task performance) is lower than indicator A (perceived task importance), then there is a training need. Paired t-test was used to determine sgnificance in dfferences between each category pair
